Our Story

Building the future of tourism in Ghana, one journey at a time.

Who We Are

LonAsh Tours is a newly launched, forward-thinking tourism company based in the historic city of Cape Coast, Ghana. Founded by Benjamin Eyison, our company was born out of a deep passion for showcasing the hidden gems, rich culture, and breathtaking landscapes of West Africa to the world.

As a new startup, we bring a fresh perspective to the tourism industry. We aren't just another tour agency; we are your local partners in adventure. We believe that travel should be more than just visiting a place—it should be about connecting with people, understanding history, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, sustainable, and high-quality tourism experiences that celebrate Ghana's heritage while empowering local communities.

Our Vision

To become the leading tourism platform in West Africa, recognized for our excellence in service, innovation, and commitment to cultural preservation.

Why We Started

We noticed a gap in the market for personalized, budget-friendly, yet professional tourism services that cater to both locals and international visitors. LonAsh Tours was established to bridge this gap, offering everything from group travel for schools and churches to luxury vacation planning for families and couples.

Based at 91 Kotokuraba Rd in Cape Coast, we are at the heart of Ghana's tourism hub, perfectly positioned to guide you through the history of the Cape Coast and Elmina Castles, the heights of Kakum National Park, and beyond.
